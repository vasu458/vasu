Food waste is a significant global issue, with approximately one-third of all food produced being discarded each year. Many individuals and households struggle to manage their leftovers, often forgetting about them until they spoil.
This not only contributes to environmental degradation but also leads to unnecessary financial loss.
Existing solutions do not adequately address the need for timely reminders, community sharing options, or education on food preservation.
Therefore, there is a pressing need for an intuitive app that notifies users about their leftovers, encourages sharing within local communities, and provides resources for effective food management, ultimately reducing food waste and promoting sustainable consumption practices.
